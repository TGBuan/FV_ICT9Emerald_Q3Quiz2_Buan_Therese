Synopsis Cast and Characters Movie Review
The four sisters reunited to discourage their younger brother’s marriage. As they interact, they will face the feelings and issues that were kept buried for a long time. Toni Gonzaga as Teddie Salazar, Bea Alonzo as Bobbie Salazar, Angel Locsin as Alexandra Salazar, Shaina Magdayao as Gabbie Salazar, & Enchong Dee as CJ Salazar ROSALES, KOBE JOECEL V.: "Ever since this movie was released up until now, this movie's still my most-watched and most-favorite movie ever. Because from the storyline, we can see how the four sisters teamed up to sabotage their younger brother's wedding. But rather than sticking and focusing with their plan, it implies that their main target is to fix their trust, love, and care for each other. And also, I realized that we should learn how to accept someone even though he/she's not your favorite/type of the family. And we shouldn't biased someone because we know how it feels to be biased by anyone and it deeply hurts inside. And because if we're feeling biased, sometimes, we feel left out from others and be introverted. That's why we shouldn't lose our trust and care to anyone who's important in our lives just because of their wrongdoings, let's all learn how to accept and forgive someone's mistakes even if it's intentional. This is one of the movies which can turn into a reality story that may happen in our society. Since this is a family comedy movie, for me, this is a well-deserving movie to be praised by viewers. It's completely legit and heartfelt movie I've ever seen in my entire life.”
A young couple who has a dream growing up old together as they deal with their struggles of being in a long-term relationship. Kathryn Bernado as George & Daniel Pandilla as Primo Vins Khandagale: “This is one of my most favourite romantic movies. A love story came out of mature mind.10/10. In one way, it isn't a movie, instead it's like watching real life experiences of couple who become wise with the life experiences. This movie slowly dragged me in, and I became an invisible character in movie, watching everything as it happens. Story is simple yet amazing. In such type of plots, movie makers are prone to fall for cliche melodramatic tricks to make her fall for him again. But it's not the case here. We can understand both the characters's point of views. Both the actors are adorable, they performed superbly, lovely cinematography. Salute to story and dialogue writer. All the song lyrics are in accordance with situation.”
The Siblings reunite as they hear their father is diagnosed with cancer, and they must settle their old issues as they spend time together. Ronaldo Valdez as Manuel Bonifacio, Aga Muhlach as Allan Bonifacio, Dingdong Dantes as Bryan Bonifacio, Enrique Gil as Dexter Bonifacio & Cristine Reyes as Cha Bonifacio Zamantha Dela Cruz: “I love the film. It signifies the shortcomings from a family in real life. The film brought a nostalgic and memorable life lessons that actually indicates that we should be grateful for our family especially our parents that we should never take them for granted. The film allows the viewers to look back on painful dispute from a family that oftentimes can lead to hatred due to imperfections from the lives of different family. The film was exceptionally great and inspiring cause it actually expresses that every arguments that a family hvae to deal with would always end up finding hope and forgiveness.”
